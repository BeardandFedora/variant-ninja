Is the monthly access time-limited?

No, you can learn at your own pace and you will have full access to all labs and course material. We add new content monthly so there's always something to come back for!

Can I tweak some of the content of the labs?

All our labs are built to support international curriculums and even though some experiments in our labs are not exactly what you are use to, all learning outcomes should support curriculum standards.

Do I need Oculus Rift?

No, but it helps! Studies have shown that the most appealing virtual reality is 3D, and Labster features a sleek, 3D environment. You will also get extra street cred for using a Oculus Rift for school.

Can I download the course material in PDF?

No need - you can access the course content at any time from any device using our online learning hub.
